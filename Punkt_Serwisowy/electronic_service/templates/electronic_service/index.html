How does it work?
  1. Registration
    First create an account, then make a request where you will add all your faulty devices
  2. Delivery of the device
    After making request you will recive e-mail from our delivery partner with all information about sending us your devices
  3. Diagnosis
    After a precise diagnosis of the device, we contact you to provide detailed information about the repair process and present a cost estimate.
  4. Devices repair
    After accepting the costs, we proceed to repair. The device is then tested again. A fully functional device is ready for courier shipment to the address provided in the service request.
